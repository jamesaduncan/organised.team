  • Business Architecture

    • Business architecture involves understanding, documenting and designing the structure of an organisation, including its business processes, capabilities, information flows, and organizational structure to support strategic objectives.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand what business architecture is and how it supports organisational objectives. You recognise the value of business architecture in transformation projects.

    2. Working

      You can apply business architecture principles and create basic business architecture artefacts under guidance. You can identify business capabilities and map simple processes.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design and create comprehensive business architecture models, lead business architecture activities in projects, and provide guidance to others on business architecture approaches.

    4. Expert

      You can establish business architecture strategy and standards, lead complex organisational transformation through business architecture, and mentor others in advanced business architecture practices.
