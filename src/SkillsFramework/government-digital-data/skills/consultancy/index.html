  • Consultancy

    • Consultancy involves providing expert advice, guidance and recommendations to help organisations solve problems, improve performance, and achieve their objectives through structured analysis and stakeholder engagement.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the role of consultancy in problem-solving and recognise when expert advice is needed to address organisational challenges.

    2. Working

      You can provide basic advice and recommendations based on analysis, and support consultancy engagements by gathering information and presenting findings.

    3. Practitioner

      You can lead consultancy engagements, structure complex problems, develop evidence-based recommendations, and influence stakeholders to adopt solutions.

    4. Expert

      You can establish consultancy approaches and methodologies, lead complex organisational transformation as a trusted advisor, and mentor others in consultancy skills.
