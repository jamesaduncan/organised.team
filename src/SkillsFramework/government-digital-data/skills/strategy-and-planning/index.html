  • Strategy and Planning

    • Strategy and planning involves developing long-term direction, setting objectives, analysing environments and options, and creating actionable plans to achieve organisational goals and respond to challenges and opportunities.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand strategic thinking concepts and recognise the importance of planning in achieving organisational objectives. You can contribute to planning activities.

    2. Working

      You can develop tactical plans, conduct basic environmental analysis, and support strategic planning processes by gathering information and analysing options.

    3. Practitioner

      You can lead strategic planning processes, conduct comprehensive environmental scanning, develop strategic options, and create detailed implementation plans with clear objectives and measures.

    4. Expert

      You can establish strategic planning methodologies, lead organisation-wide strategic transformation, influence strategic direction at senior levels, and mentor others in strategic thinking.
