  • Senior Digital Portfolio Manager

    • A senior digital portfolio manager leads complex, strategic portfolio management activities and provides expert guidance on portfolio strategy. They mentor portfolio managers and work with senior leadership to drive digital transformation and innovation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead strategic portfolio management across complex digital ecosystems
    • Develop portfolio strategy aligned with organisational transformation goals
    • Mentor digital portfolio managers and establish best practices
    • Work with senior leadership on digital investment strategies
    • Drive innovation and continuous improvement in portfolio practices
    • Manage complex dependencies and risks across large-scale portfolios
    • Influence digital strategy and policy through portfolio insights

    Required Skills

    • Product Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Mentoring Working
    • Data Analysis Working
    • Problem Management Working
