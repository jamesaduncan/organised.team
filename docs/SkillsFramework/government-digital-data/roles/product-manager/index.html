  • Product Manager

    • A product manager is responsible for the strategy, roadmap, and feature definition of a product or product area. They work closely with engineering, design, and business teams to deliver products that meet user needs and business objectives.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define product strategy, vision, and roadmap
    • Gather and prioritise product requirements from stakeholders
    • Work closely with engineering and design teams to deliver products
    • Conduct user research and market analysis to inform product decisions
    • Measure and analyse product performance and user feedback
    • Manage stakeholder expectations and communicate product updates
    • Ensure products meet accessibility and usability standards
