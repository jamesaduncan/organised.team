  • Senior Solution Architect

    • A senior solution architect leads complex solution transformation initiatives and provides strategic direction on solution architecture. They influence organisation-wide solution decisions and serve as a senior subject matter expert on solution design and technical delivery.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead enterprise-wide solution transformation programmes and strategic initiatives
    • Define organisation solution strategy and target solution architecture
    • Establish solution architecture governance frameworks and standards
    • Lead complex stakeholder engagement across multiple business areas for solution delivery
    • Provide expert consultation on strategic solution technology decisions
    • Mentor and develop solution architecture capability across the organisation
    • Drive innovation and evaluation of emerging solution technologies
    • Lead organisation-wide solution integration and platform strategies
    • Collaborate with enterprise architects on cross-cutting solution concerns

    Required Skills

    • Solution Architecture Expert
    • Technical Design Expert
    • Integration Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Enterprise Architecture Working
    • Software Development Working
