  • Head of Digital Portfolio

    • A head of digital portfolio is responsible for organisational digital portfolio strategy and leads teams of portfolio managers. They work at executive level to drive digital transformation, establish portfolio governance, and influence strategic investment decisions.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisational digital portfolio strategy
    • Lead and manage teams of digital portfolio managers
    • Work with executive leadership on digital transformation strategy
    • Establish portfolio governance and investment frameworks
    • Drive innovation and best practice in portfolio management
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through portfolio insights
    • Manage organisational digital investment and value realisation

    Required Skills

    • Product Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • People Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Data Analysis Working
