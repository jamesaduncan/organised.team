  • Head of Service

    • A head of service is responsible for organisational service strategy and leads teams of service owners. They work at executive level to drive service transformation, establish service standards, and influence strategic direction through service excellence.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisational service strategy and standards
    • Lead and manage teams of service owners across multiple domains
    • Work with executive leadership on service transformation strategy
    • Establish service governance and performance frameworks
    • Drive innovation and best practice in service delivery
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through service insights
    • Manage organisational service capability and transformation

    Required Skills

    • Product Management Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • People Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Data Analysis Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Service Owner

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • SCS2 (Senior Civil Service Pay Band 2)
    • SCS3 (Senior Civil Service Pay Band 3)
