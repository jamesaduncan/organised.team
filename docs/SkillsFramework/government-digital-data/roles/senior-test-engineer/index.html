  • Senior Test Engineer

    • A senior test engineer leads complex testing engineering initiatives and provides expert technical guidance on test automation and quality engineering practices. They architect testing solutions, mentor junior engineers, and drive continuous improvement in testing capabilities.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Architect and lead complex test automation and quality engineering solutions
    • Design enterprise-level testing frameworks and infrastructure
    • Lead technical innovation in testing tools and practices
    • Mentor junior test engineers and provide technical leadership
    • Establish testing engineering standards and best practices
    • Collaborate with senior stakeholders on quality engineering strategy
    • Drive adoption of emerging testing technologies and methodologies

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Expert
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Mentoring Working
    • Accessibility Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
