  • Senior Delivery Manager

    • A senior delivery manager leads complex, high-risk delivery programmes and provides expert guidance on delivery practices. They mentor delivery managers and work with senior stakeholders to ensure successful delivery of strategic initiatives.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex, multi-team delivery programmes and initiatives
    • Establish delivery standards and best practices across programmes
    • Mentor delivery managers and provide expert delivery guidance
    • Work with senior stakeholders on strategic delivery planning
    • Manage dependencies and risks across multiple delivery streams
    • Drive continuous improvement in delivery capabilities
    • Lead delivery transformation and change initiatives

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • People Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Mentoring Working
    • User Focus Working
    • Product Management Working
