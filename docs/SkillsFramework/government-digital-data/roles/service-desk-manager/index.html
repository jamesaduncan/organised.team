  • Service Desk Manager

    • A service desk manager oversees the delivery of first-line IT support services to end users. They ensure effective incident resolution, manage service desk operations, and maintain high levels of user satisfaction.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Manage service desk operations and staff scheduling
    • Ensure effective incident logging, categorisation, and resolution
    • Monitor service desk performance metrics and user satisfaction
    • Coordinate escalation procedures and specialist team engagement
    • Manage service desk knowledge base and documentation
    • Support user training and communication initiatives
    • Drive continuous improvement in service desk processes

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Working
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Problem Management Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Mentoring Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
