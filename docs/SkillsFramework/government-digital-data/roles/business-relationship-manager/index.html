  • Business Relationship Manager

    • A business relationship manager acts as a liaison between IT services and business stakeholders. They ensure IT services align with business needs, manage service expectations, and facilitate effective communication between technical and business teams.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Build and maintain relationships with key business stakeholders
    • Understand business requirements and translate them into IT service needs
    • Manage service level agreements and ensure service delivery meets expectations
    • Facilitate communication between IT teams and business users
    • Identify opportunities for IT service improvements and business value
    • Support business continuity and disaster recovery planning
    • Report on service performance and business impact

    Required Skills

    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • User Focus Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
