  • Principal Performance Analyst

    • A principal performance analyst provides expert leadership in organisational performance measurement and improvement. They define performance strategy, establish enterprise-wide performance capabilities, and drive transformational change in how performance is measured and managed.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define strategic vision and approach for organisational performance measurement
    • Lead enterprise-wide performance transformation initiatives
    • Establish performance management as a core organisational capability
    • Provide expert advice to executive leadership on performance strategy
    • Drive innovation in performance measurement methodologies and tools
    • Build performance analysis capability and talent development programmes
    • Represent the organisation in performance management communities and best practice networks

    Required Skills

    • Statistical Methods Expert
    • Business Analysis Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Consultancy Practitioner
