  • Senior Performance Analyst

    • A senior performance analyst leads complex performance analysis projects and establishes performance measurement capabilities across the organisation. They provide strategic insights on organisational performance and drive performance improvement initiatives.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex performance analysis projects and strategic reviews
    • Establish organisation-wide performance measurement frameworks
    • Provide strategic advice on performance management and improvement
    • Mentor junior performance analysts and build analytical capability
    • Lead cross-functional performance improvement initiatives
    • Develop advanced analytical models for performance prediction and forecasting
    • Partner with senior stakeholders to align performance metrics with strategic objectives

    Required Skills

    • Statistical Methods Practitioner
    • Data Visualisation Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Mentoring Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Consultancy Working
