  • Senior Infrastructure Operations Engineer

    • A senior infrastructure operations engineer leads complex operational activities and establishes best practices for infrastructure operations. They mentor junior staff and drive continuous improvement in operational processes.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex infrastructure operations and incident resolution
    • Establish operational best practices and procedures
    • Mentor and develop junior operations engineers
    • Drive automation and efficiency improvements in operations
    • Lead capacity planning and performance optimization initiatives
    • Collaborate with architects on operational requirements
    • Support strategic planning for operational capabilities
