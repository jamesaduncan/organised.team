  • Security Architect

    • A security architect designs and implements security solutions that meet business requirements and regulatory compliance. They work independently on complex security architecture challenges while providing guidance to associate security architects and collaborating with other architecture disciplines.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design comprehensive security architecture solutions for complex business requirements
    • Develop security standards, policies, and architectural principles
    • Lead security risk assessments and threat modelling activities
    • Evaluate and recommend security technologies and vendor solutions
    • Collaborate with other architects to ensure security is embedded across all architecture domains
    • Mentor associate security architects and provide technical guidance
    • Lead security transformation projects and compliance initiatives
    • Conduct security design reviews and provide architectural assurance
