  • Lead DevOps Engineer

    • A lead DevOps engineer provides strategic leadership and technical direction for DevOps practices across the organisation. They define standards, lead major transformation initiatives, and work closely with senior leadership to align technology strategy with business objectives.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisation-wide DevOps strategy and standards
    • Lead major infrastructure and platform transformation programmes
    • Provide technical leadership and direction to multiple DevOps teams
    • Drive continuous improvement initiatives across development and operations
    • Collaborate with enterprise architects on technology strategy
    • Champion cloud adoption and digital transformation initiatives
    • Represent the organisation at industry events and communities
    • Manage relationships with senior stakeholders and external partners

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Expert
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Consultancy Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Problem Management Practitioner
