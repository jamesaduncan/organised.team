  • Quality Assurance Test Analyst

    • A quality assurance test analyst designs, develops, and executes test strategies and plans to ensure software quality. They identify defects, verify functionality, and work with development teams to improve software reliability and user experience.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design and develop comprehensive test plans and test cases
    • Execute manual and automated testing across different testing phases
    • Identify, document, and track software defects and issues
    • Collaborate with developers and business analysts to understand requirements
    • Perform functional, integration, and user acceptance testing
    • Report on test results and provide quality assurance metrics
    • Support test automation initiatives and maintain test scripts
