  • Head of Programme Delivery

    • A head of programme delivery is responsible for organisational programme delivery strategy and leads teams of programme managers. They work at executive level to drive delivery transformation, establish governance frameworks, and influence strategic direction.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisational programme delivery strategy
    • Lead and manage teams of programme delivery managers
    • Work with executive leadership on strategic transformation initiatives
    • Establish programme governance and delivery frameworks
    • Drive innovation and best practice in programme delivery
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through delivery expertise
    • Manage organisational delivery capability and transformation

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • People Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Problem Management Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Product Management Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Programme Delivery Manager
