  • Head of Change and Release Management

    • The head of change and release management provides strategic leadership for all change and release activities across the organisation. They establish enterprise-wide policies, drive digital transformation initiatives, and ensure operational excellence in service delivery.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for change and release management across the organisation
    • Establish enterprise-wide change management policies and governance frameworks
    • Drive digital transformation and DevOps adoption initiatives
    • Lead organisational change capability development and maturity improvement
    • Represent change management in senior leadership and board-level discussions
    • Ensure alignment with business strategy and regulatory requirements
    • Drive innovation and continuous improvement in operational practices

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Problem Management Expert
    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Consultancy Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Programming and Build Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Change and Release Manager

    Next role: Chief Information Officer or similar senior executive roles

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • SCS1 (Senior Civil Service 1)
    • SCS2 (Senior Civil Service 2)
