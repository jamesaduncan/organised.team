  • Head of Service Desk Management

    • The head of service desk management provides strategic leadership for all user support activities across the organisation. They establish enterprise-wide service desk frameworks and ensure exceptional user experiences across all IT services.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for service desk operations across the organisation
    • Establish enterprise-wide service desk frameworks and governance
    • Drive digital transformation of user support capabilities
    • Lead user experience strategy and customer satisfaction initiatives
    • Represent user support interests in executive forums
    • Ensure alignment with accessibility and inclusion requirements
    • Develop strategic partnerships for user support innovation

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Consultancy Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Accessibility Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Service Desk Manager

    Next role: Chief Information Officer or similar senior executive roles

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • SCS1 (Senior Civil Service 1)
    • SCS2 (Senior Civil Service 2)
