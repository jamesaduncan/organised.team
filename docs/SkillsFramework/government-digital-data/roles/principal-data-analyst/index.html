  • Principal Data Analyst

    • A principal data analyst provides expert analytical leadership across the organisation. They define analytical strategy, lead major analytical initiatives, and drive innovation in analytical approaches and methodologies.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and execute organisation-wide analytical strategy and vision
    • Lead major analytical programmes and cross-departmental initiatives
    • Establish analytical excellence standards and governance frameworks
    • Provide expert analytical advice to senior leadership and executives
    • Drive innovation in analytical methods and technology adoption
    • Build analytical capability and lead talent development programmes
    • Represent the organisation in analytical communities and external partnerships

    Required Skills

    • Statistical Methods Expert
    • Data Visualisation Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Practitioner
