  • Head of Test

    • A head of test is responsible for testing strategy and quality assurance across an entire organisation. They establish organisational testing capability, lead transformation in quality practices, and work at executive level to ensure digital services meet the highest standards of quality and user experience.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisational testing strategy and quality frameworks
    • Lead testing capability transformation and establish quality maturity
    • Work with executive leadership on strategic quality and risk initiatives
    • Manage organisational testing budgets, resources, and capability development
    • Drive innovation and continuous improvement in testing and quality practices
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through testing and quality expertise
    • Establish organisational testing standards and governance frameworks

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Programming and Build Working

    Previous role: Senior Test Manager
