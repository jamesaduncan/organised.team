  • Senior Change and Release Manager

    • A senior change and release manager leads complex change and release management activities and establishes best practices across multiple services. They drive continuous improvement in change processes and mentor junior staff.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex change and release management initiatives across multiple services
    • Establish and continuously improve change management processes and policies
    • Mentor and develop junior change and release managers
    • Drive automation and efficiency improvements in release processes
    • Lead change advisory board meetings and governance activities
    • Collaborate with architects on service design for operational effectiveness
    • Support strategic planning for change and release capabilities

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Expert
    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Leadership Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Programming and Build Working
