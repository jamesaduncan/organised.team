  • Associate Solution Architect

    • An associate solution architect works under guidance to support solution architecture activities. They help design technical solutions and contribute to solution planning while developing their expertise in solution design and technical delivery.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Support the development of solution architecture designs and documentation
    • Assist in solution options analysis and technology selection
    • Help evaluate technical solutions and integration approaches
    • Contribute to solution design reviews and technical assessments
    • Support solution delivery and implementation activities
    • Assist with solution testing and quality assurance activities

    Required Skills

    • Solution Architecture Working
    • Technical Design Working
    • Integration Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Enterprise Architecture Awareness
