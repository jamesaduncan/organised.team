  • Senior Data Ethicist

    • A senior data ethicist leads the development and implementation of comprehensive data ethics frameworks across the organisation. They provide expert guidance on complex ethical issues and establish organisation-wide standards for responsible data use.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead development of organisation-wide data ethics strategy and policies
    • Provide expert consultation on complex ethical and regulatory challenges
    • Establish ethical review processes and governance frameworks
    • Lead cross-functional teams in addressing ethical data issues
    • Develop training programmes and capability building initiatives
    • Represent the organisation in regulatory discussions and industry forums
    • Monitor emerging ethical issues and regulatory developments

    Required Skills

    • Data Ethics and Privacy Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Consultancy Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Leadership Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • User Research Working
