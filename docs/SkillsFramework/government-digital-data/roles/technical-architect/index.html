  • Technical Architect

    • A technical architect designs and implements technical solutions that meet functional and non-functional requirements. They work independently on complex technical architecture challenges while providing guidance to associate technical architects and collaborating with development teams.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design comprehensive technical architectures for complex systems and applications
    • Develop technical standards, patterns, and architectural principles
    • Lead technical options analysis and technology selection processes
    • Evaluate and recommend technical solutions and development approaches
    • Collaborate with development teams to ensure technical implementation quality
    • Mentor associate technical architects and provide technical guidance
    • Lead technical delivery projects and development initiatives
    • Conduct technical design reviews and provide architectural assurance
