  • Analytics Engineer

    • An analytics engineer transforms raw data into clean, reliable datasets and models that enable business analysis and reporting. They bridge the gap between data engineering and analytics, ensuring data quality and accessibility for business users.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design and build data transformation pipelines using tools like dbt or similar
    • Create and maintain data models that support business intelligence and reporting needs
    • Implement data quality checks and monitoring to ensure data reliability
    • Collaborate with analysts and stakeholders to understand data requirements
    • Document data lineage and maintain data catalogues for business users
    • Optimise query performance and data warehouse efficiency

    Required Skills

    • Data Engineering Working
    • Programming and Build Working
    • Statistical Methods Working
    • Data Visualisation Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Working
