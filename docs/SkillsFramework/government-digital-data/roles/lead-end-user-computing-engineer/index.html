  • Lead End User Computing Engineer

    • A lead end user computing engineer provides strategic leadership for all end-user computing activities across the organisation. They establish enterprise-wide standards, drive innovation in user computing experiences, and ensure alignment with business objectives.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for end-user computing across the organisation
    • Establish enterprise-wide standards and frameworks for user computing
    • Drive innovation and digital workplace transformation initiatives
    • Lead cross-functional initiatives to improve user productivity and experience
    • Represent end-user computing interests in senior leadership forums
    • Develop strategic partnerships with technology vendors and service providers
    • Ensure alignment between user computing strategy and business objectives

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Consultancy Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Accessibility Practitioner

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior End User Computing Engineer

    Next role: Chief Information Officer or similar senior executive roles

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • G6 (Grade 6)
    • SCS1 (Senior Civil Service 1)
