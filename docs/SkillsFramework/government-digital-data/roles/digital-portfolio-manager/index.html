  • Digital Portfolio Manager

    • A digital portfolio manager is responsible for managing a portfolio of digital products and services, ensuring strategic alignment and optimal resource allocation. They coordinate across multiple product teams to deliver coherent user experiences and business value.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Manage portfolio of digital products and services strategically
    • Coordinate dependencies and resource allocation across product teams
    • Ensure portfolio alignment with organisational strategy and user needs
    • Monitor and report on portfolio performance and value delivery
    • Work with stakeholders to prioritise portfolio investments
    • Support product teams with strategic guidance and direction
    • Manage portfolio risks and ensure compliance with standards

    Required Skills

    • Product Management Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • User Focus Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Data Analysis Working
    • Problem Management Working
