  • Associate Product Manager

    • An associate product manager supports product management activities and works under guidance to help deliver user-centred products and services. They assist with research, analysis, and coordination activities while developing product management skills.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Support product research and user needs analysis
    • Assist with product backlog management and prioritisation
    • Help coordinate product delivery activities with development teams
    • Support stakeholder engagement and communication activities
    • Contribute to product planning and roadmap development
    • Assist with product performance monitoring and reporting
    • Support user testing and feedback collection activities

    Required Skills

    • Product Management Awareness
    • User Focus Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Awareness
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Data Analysis Awareness
    • Problem Management Awareness
    • Strategy and Planning Awareness
