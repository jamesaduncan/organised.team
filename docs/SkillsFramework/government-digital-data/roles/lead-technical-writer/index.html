  • Lead Technical Writer

    • A lead technical writer leads the technical communication function across the organisation, setting strategic direction for documentation and technical writing approaches. They manage technical writing teams and drive organisational excellence in technical communication and knowledge management.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement technical writing strategy across the entire organisation
    • Lead and manage technical writing teams and documentation specialists
    • Establish technical communication governance, standards, and frameworks
    • Champion user-centred documentation approaches at executive level
    • Build strategic partnerships with technical communication communities
    • Oversee documentation capability development and knowledge management systems
    • Drive innovation in technical writing tools, technologies, and methodologies
    • Ensure technical communication excellence across all organisational programmes

    Required Skills

    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • User Research Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
