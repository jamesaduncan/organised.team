  • Senior Service Designer

    • A senior service designer leads service design for complex programmes and organisational transformation. They mentor junior designers, establish service design standards, and drive service design excellence while ensuring user needs are met through comprehensive, accessible service experiences.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead service design for complex, multi-agency programmes and transformations
    • Develop service design strategies and frameworks for organisational adoption
    • Mentor and develop junior service designers and service design capability
    • Establish service design standards, methods, and best practices
    • Lead cross-organisational collaboration on service transformation
    • Drive innovation in service design tools, techniques, and approaches
    • Provide expert guidance on complex service challenges and policy implications

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Expert
    • User Research Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
