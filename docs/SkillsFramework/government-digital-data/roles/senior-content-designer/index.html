  • Senior Content Designer

    • A senior content designer leads content design for complex digital services and programmes. They mentor junior content designers, establish content strategies at scale, and drive content design practices across multidisciplinary teams while ensuring user needs are met through exceptional content experiences.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead content design for complex, multi-service digital programmes
    • Develop content strategies that span multiple touchpoints and channels
    • Mentor and develop junior content designers and team members
    • Establish content design standards and governance across teams
    • Lead cross-functional collaboration to embed content thinking in service design
    • Drive content design innovation and best practice adoption
    • Provide expert guidance on complex content architecture and information design

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • User Research Practitioner
    • Accessibility Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
