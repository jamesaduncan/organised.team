  • Associate Enterprise Architect

    • An associate enterprise architect works under guidance to support enterprise architecture activities. They help analyse business capabilities and contribute to enterprise architecture artefacts while developing their understanding of the organisation's strategic direction.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Support the development of enterprise architecture artefacts and documentation
    • Assist in business capability modelling and gap analysis
    • Help evaluate technology solutions against enterprise standards
    • Contribute to architecture governance and review processes
    • Support stakeholder engagement on architecture matters

    Required Skills

    • Enterprise Architecture Working
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Strategy and Planning Awareness
    • Problem Management Awareness
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Awareness
