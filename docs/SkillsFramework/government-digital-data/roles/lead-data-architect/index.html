  • Lead Data Architect

    • A lead data architect provides strategic leadership for data architecture across the organisation. They shape the organisation's data strategy, lead major transformation initiatives, and build high-performing data architecture teams.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and execute organisation-wide data architecture vision and strategy
    • Lead major data transformation programmes and strategic initiatives
    • Build and lead high-performing data architecture teams across multiple locations
    • Influence senior leadership on data architecture investment decisions
    • Establish governance frameworks and architecture review processes
    • Drive innovation in data architecture and emerging technologies
    • Represent the organisation in external data architecture forums and communities

    Required Skills

    • Data Architecture Expert
    • Strategic Thinking Expert
    • Leadership and Guidance Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Data Governance Expert
    • Consultancy Practitioner
    • Innovation and Emerging Technology Practitioner

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Data Architect
