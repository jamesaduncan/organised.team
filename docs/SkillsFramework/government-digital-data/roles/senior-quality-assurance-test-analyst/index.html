  • Senior Quality Assurance Test Analyst

    • A senior quality assurance test analyst leads complex testing initiatives and provides expert guidance on quality assurance practices. They mentor junior testers, establish testing standards, and drive continuous improvement in testing processes across projects.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex testing projects and establish comprehensive test strategies
    • Design and implement advanced test automation frameworks
    • Mentor junior test analysts and provide technical guidance
    • Establish testing standards and quality assurance best practices
    • Collaborate with senior stakeholders on quality requirements and risk assessment
    • Drive continuous improvement in testing processes and tools
    • Lead cross-team testing initiatives and integration testing efforts
