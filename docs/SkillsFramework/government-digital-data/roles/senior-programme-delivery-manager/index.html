  • Senior Programme Delivery Manager

    • A senior programme delivery manager leads high-complexity, strategic programmes and provides expert guidance on programme delivery. They mentor programme managers and work with senior leadership to deliver transformation initiatives with significant organisational impact.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead strategic, high-complexity transformation programmes
    • Establish programme delivery standards and best practices
    • Mentor programme and delivery managers across the organisation
    • Work with senior leadership on strategic programme planning
    • Drive organisational change and capability transformation
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through programme insights
    • Lead cross-organisational collaboration and partnership initiatives

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • People Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Problem Management Expert
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • User Focus Working
    • Product Management Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Programme Delivery Manager

    Next role: Head of Programme Delivery

    Indicative Civil Service grades
