  • Head of Product

    • A head of product is responsible for organisational product strategy and leads teams of product managers. They work at executive level to drive product innovation, establish product standards, and influence strategic direction through product expertise.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisational product strategy and vision
    • Lead and manage teams of product managers across multiple domains
    • Work with executive leadership on strategic product decisions
    • Establish product standards, methodologies, and best practices
    • Drive innovation and transformation in product capabilities
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through product insights
    • Manage organisational product portfolio and investment decisions

    Required Skills

    • Product Management Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • People Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Data Analysis Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Product Manager

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • G6 (Grade 6)
    • SCS1 (Senior Civil Service Pay Band 1)
    • SCS2 (Senior Civil Service Pay Band 2)
