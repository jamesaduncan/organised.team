  • Business Analyst

    • A business analyst analyses business processes and requirements to improve services and products. They work with stakeholders to gather requirements, map processes, and support the delivery of digital solutions that meet user needs.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Gather, document, and analyse business requirements
    • Create process maps, user stories, and functional specifications
    • Facilitate workshops and requirements gathering sessions
    • Support user research and usability testing activities
    • Analyse data to identify trends and insights for business decisions
    • Work with delivery teams to ensure requirements are understood and delivered
    • Support change management and process improvement initiatives
