  • Principal Data Scientist

    • A principal data scientist provides technical leadership and strategic direction for data science across the organisation. They lead complex research initiatives, mentor teams, and drive innovation in data science methodologies and applications.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead strategic data science initiatives and complex research programmes
    • Develop and implement advanced analytical methodologies and models
    • Provide technical leadership and mentoring to data science teams
    • Drive innovation in data science tools, techniques, and applications
    • Collaborate with senior stakeholders on data-driven strategic decisions
    • Establish data science standards and best practices
    • Represent the organisation in external data science communities

    Required Skills

    • Statistical Methods Expert
    • Machine Learning Expert
    • Programming and Build Expert
    • Leadership and Guidance Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Data Visualisation Practitioner
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Expert
    • Research Methods Expert

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Data Scientist
