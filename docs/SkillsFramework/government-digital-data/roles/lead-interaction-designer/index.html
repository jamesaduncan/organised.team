  • Lead Interaction Designer

    • A lead interaction designer leads the interaction design function across the organisation, setting strategic direction for user experience approaches and ensuring user-centred design principles are embedded throughout all digital services. They manage interaction design teams and drive organisational UX excellence.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement interaction design strategy across the entire organisation
    • Lead and manage interaction design teams and UX specialists
    • Establish interaction design governance, standards, and design systems
    • Champion user-centred design approaches at executive level
    • Build strategic partnerships with design and UX communities
    • Oversee UX capability development and training programmes
    • Drive innovation in interaction design tools, techniques, and methodologies
    • Ensure user experience excellence across all organisational programmes

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • User Research Expert
    • Accessibility Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
