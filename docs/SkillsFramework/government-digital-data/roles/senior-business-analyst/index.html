  • Senior Business Analyst

    • A senior business analyst leads complex business analysis activities and provides expert guidance on requirements and process improvement. They mentor junior analysts and work with senior stakeholders to deliver strategic business solutions.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex business analysis projects and initiatives
    • Develop business cases and strategic recommendations
    • Mentor junior business analysts and provide technical guidance
    • Establish business analysis standards and methodologies
    • Work with senior stakeholders to influence strategy and policy
    • Lead cross-functional workshops and strategic planning sessions
    • Drive process transformation and improvement initiatives

    Required Skills

    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Problem Management Working
    • Data Analysis Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Mentoring Working
