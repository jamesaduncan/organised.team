  • Business Architect

    • A business architect uses their knowledge and experience to understand business scenarios, communicate issues and recommend next steps. They support and guide other business architects while working independently on complex business architecture challenges.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Create business architecture artefacts, including abstract representations of the organisation
    • Develop standard and scalable models to collect, aggregate, or disaggregate complex information
    • Apply architectural principles, methods, and tools to business scenarios
    • Lead stakeholder engagement and facilitate complex business discussions
    • Provide consultancy and advice on business architecture matters
    • Mentor junior business architects and support their development
    • Collaborate with enterprise and technical architects on cross-cutting concerns

    Required Skills

    • Business Architecture Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • Enterprise Architecture Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
