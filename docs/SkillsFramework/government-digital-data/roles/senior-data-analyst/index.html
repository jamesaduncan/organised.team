  • Senior Data Analyst

    • A senior data analyst leads complex analytical projects and provides expert guidance on data-driven decision making. They mentor junior analysts and establish analytical standards and best practices across their domain.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex analytical projects and drive strategic insights
    • Develop advanced analytical models and methodologies
    • Mentor junior analysts and provide technical guidance
    • Establish analytical standards and quality assurance processes
    • Partner with senior stakeholders to influence policy and strategy
    • Design and implement analytical frameworks for business areas
    • Lead cross-functional analytical initiatives and collaborations

    Required Skills

    • Statistical Methods Practitioner
    • Data Visualisation Practitioner
    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
