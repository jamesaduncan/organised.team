  • Solution Architect

    • A solution architect designs and implements technical solutions that meet business requirements and integration needs. They work independently on complex solution architecture challenges while providing guidance to associate solution architects and collaborating with other architecture disciplines.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design comprehensive solution architectures for complex business requirements
    • Develop solution standards, patterns, and architectural principles
    • Lead solution options analysis and technology selection processes
    • Evaluate and recommend technical solutions and integration approaches
    • Collaborate with other architects to ensure solution alignment with enterprise architecture
    • Mentor associate solution architects and provide technical guidance
    • Lead solution delivery projects and technical implementations
    • Conduct solution design reviews and provide architectural assurance
