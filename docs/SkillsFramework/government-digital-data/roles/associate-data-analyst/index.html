  • Associate Data Analyst

    • An associate data analyst works with data to answer business questions and support decision-making. They continue to receive guidance while developing their analytical skills and understanding of business contexts.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Conduct basic data analysis using statistical tools and techniques
    • Create simple reports and visualisations to communicate findings
    • Clean and prepare data for analysis under guidance
    • Support senior analysts in larger analytical projects
    • Learn to interpret business requirements and translate them into analytical questions
    • Maintain data quality and document analytical processes

    Required Skills

    • Statistical Methods Awareness
    • Data Visualisation Working
    • Programming and Build Awareness
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Awareness
    • User Focus Awareness
