  • Senior Enterprise Architect

    • A senior enterprise architect leads complex enterprise transformation initiatives and provides strategic direction on technology architecture. They influence organisation-wide technology decisions and serve as a senior subject matter expert.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead enterprise-wide digital transformation programmes
    • Define organisation technology strategy and target architecture
    • Establish architecture governance frameworks and standards
    • Lead complex stakeholder engagement across multiple business areas
    • Provide expert consultation on strategic technology decisions
    • Mentor and develop enterprise architecture capability
    • Drive innovation and evaluation of emerging technologies

    Required Skills

    • Enterprise Architecture Expert
    • Strategic Thinking Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Leadership and Guidance Practitioner
    • Technology Strategy Practitioner
    • Consultancy Practitioner
    • Governance Practitioner
