  • Senior IT Service Manager

    • A senior IT service manager leads complex service delivery activities and establishes best practices for service management across multiple services. They mentor junior service managers and drive strategic service improvements.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex service delivery initiatives across multiple services
    • Establish service management best practices and standards
    • Mentor and develop junior service managers
    • Drive service innovation and digital transformation initiatives
    • Lead strategic stakeholder relationships at senior level
    • Support service portfolio management and investment decisions
    • Collaborate with architects on service design and strategy

    Required Skills

    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Leadership Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Consultancy Practitioner
    • User Focus Practitioner
