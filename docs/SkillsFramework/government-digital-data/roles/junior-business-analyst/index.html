  • Junior Business Analyst

    • A junior business analyst supports the analysis of business processes and requirements to help improve services and products. They work under guidance to gather requirements, document processes, and support stakeholder engagement activities.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Support requirements gathering and documentation activities
    • Assist with process mapping and analysis under supervision
    • Help facilitate workshops and stakeholder meetings
    • Create and maintain simple documentation and reports
    • Support user research and testing activities
    • Contribute to project planning and delivery activities
    • Assist with data collection and basic analysis

    Required Skills

    • Business Analysis Awareness
    • User Focus Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Awareness
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Problem Management Awareness
    • Data Analysis Awareness
