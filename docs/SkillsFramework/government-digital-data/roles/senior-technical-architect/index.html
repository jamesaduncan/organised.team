  • Senior Technical Architect

    • A senior technical architect leads complex technical transformation initiatives and provides strategic direction on technical architecture. They influence organisation-wide technical decisions and serve as a senior subject matter expert on technical design and software development practices.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead enterprise-wide technical transformation programmes and strategic initiatives
    • Define organisation technical strategy and target technical architecture
    • Establish technical architecture governance frameworks and standards
    • Lead complex stakeholder engagement across multiple development teams and business areas
    • Provide expert consultation on strategic technical architecture decisions
    • Mentor and develop technical architecture capability across the organisation
    • Drive innovation and evaluation of emerging technical technologies and practices
    • Lead organisation-wide technical platform and development strategies
    • Collaborate with enterprise architects on cross-cutting technical concerns

    Required Skills

    • Technical Architecture Expert
    • Software Development Expert
    • Technical Design Expert
    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Integration Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
