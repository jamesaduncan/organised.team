  • Lead Data Governance Manager

    • A lead data governance manager defines the strategic direction for data governance across the organisation and industry. They establish governance as a core organisational capability, influence policy development, and drive transformation in data management practices.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define strategic vision and roadmap for organisational data governance
    • Establish data governance as a core organisational capability and competency
    • Influence industry standards and best practices in data governance
    • Lead organisational transformation in data management and stewardship
    • Build strategic partnerships with regulatory bodies and industry organisations
    • Develop next-generation data governance frameworks and methodologies
    • Advise executive leadership on data governance strategy and risk management

    Required Skills

    • Data Ethics and Privacy Expert
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Consultancy Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
