  • Senior Incident Manager

    • A senior incident manager leads complex incident management activities and establishes best practices for incident response across multiple services. They mentor junior incident managers and drive continuous improvement in incident management processes.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead major incident response and coordinate enterprise-wide incident activities
    • Establish incident management processes and best practices across the organisation
    • Mentor and develop junior incident management staff
    • Drive continuous improvement in incident response capabilities
    • Lead post-incident reviews and ensure implementation of improvements
    • Collaborate with senior leadership on service resilience strategies
    • Support strategic planning for incident management capabilities

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Expert
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Consultancy Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
