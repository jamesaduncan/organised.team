  • Senior Graphic Designer

    • A senior graphic designer leads visual design for complex digital programmes and services. They mentor junior designers, establish design standards, and drive visual design excellence while ensuring accessibility and user-centred approaches are embedded throughout design processes.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead visual design for complex, multi-service digital programmes
    • Develop comprehensive design systems and brand frameworks
    • Mentor and develop junior graphic designers and visual design capability
    • Establish visual design standards and quality assurance processes
    • Lead cross-functional collaboration to embed design thinking
    • Drive innovation in visual design tools, techniques, and approaches
    • Provide expert guidance on complex accessibility and inclusion challenges

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Expert
    • Accessibility Practitioner
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • User Research Working
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
