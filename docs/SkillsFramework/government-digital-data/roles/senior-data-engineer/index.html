  • Senior Data Engineer

    • A senior data engineer leads complex data infrastructure projects and provides technical expertise in data architecture and engineering. They mentor junior engineers and establish engineering standards for data platforms and systems.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Architect and lead complex data platform and infrastructure projects
    • Design scalable data architectures and systems
    • Mentor junior data engineers and provide technical guidance
    • Establish data engineering standards and best practices
    • Lead performance optimisation and capacity planning initiatives
    • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand enterprise data requirements
    • Drive innovation in data engineering technologies and methodologies

    Required Skills

    • Data Engineering Practitioner
    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Mentoring Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • Statistical Methods Working
