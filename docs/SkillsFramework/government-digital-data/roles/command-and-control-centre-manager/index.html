  • Command and Control Centre Manager

    • A command and control centre manager oversees the centralised monitoring and coordination of IT services and operations. They ensure rapid response to incidents, coordinate operational activities, and maintain situational awareness across all critical systems.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Manage 24/7 operations centre activities and staff scheduling
    • Coordinate incident response and escalation procedures
    • Monitor critical system performance and service availability
    • Maintain operational dashboards and situational awareness tools
    • Lead operational meetings and briefings with senior stakeholders
    • Coordinate with external suppliers and service providers
    • Ensure compliance with operational procedures and governance requirements

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Working
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Mentoring Awareness
    • Consultancy Awareness
