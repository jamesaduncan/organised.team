  • Head of IT Service Management

    • The head of IT service management provides strategic leadership for all service management activities across the organisation. They establish enterprise-wide service management frameworks and ensure services consistently deliver business value.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for service management across the organisation
    • Establish enterprise-wide service management frameworks and governance
    • Drive service-oriented digital transformation initiatives
    • Lead service portfolio strategy and investment planning
    • Represent service management interests in executive forums
    • Ensure alignment between service strategy and business objectives
    • Develop strategic partnerships with service providers and vendors

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Consultancy Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Business Analysis Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Problem Management Practitioner

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior IT Service Manager

    Next role: Chief Information Officer or similar senior executive roles

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • SCS1 (Senior Civil Service 1)
    • SCS2 (Senior Civil Service 2)
