  • Senior Accessibility Specialist

    • A senior accessibility specialist leads accessibility initiatives across complex digital services and programmes. They provide strategic guidance on inclusive design, mentor junior specialists, and drive organisational change to embed accessibility practices throughout the development lifecycle.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead accessibility strategy and implementation across multiple programmes
    • Provide expert consultancy on complex accessibility challenges and solutions
    • Mentor and develop junior accessibility specialists and team members
    • Establish accessibility standards, processes, and governance frameworks
    • Lead cross-functional collaboration to embed accessibility throughout design and development
    • Represent the organisation in accessibility communities and standards bodies
    • Drive innovation in accessibility tools, techniques, and approaches

    Required Skills

    • Accessibility Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • User Research Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
