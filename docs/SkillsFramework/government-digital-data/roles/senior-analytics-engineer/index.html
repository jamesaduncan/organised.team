  • Senior Analytics Engineer

    • A senior analytics engineer leads the design and implementation of complex data transformation systems and data models. They mentor junior engineers and establish best practices for analytics engineering across the organisation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Architect and lead complex data transformation projects and analytics platforms
    • Establish data modelling standards and best practices across the organisation
    • Design scalable data architectures that support enterprise analytics needs
    • Mentor junior analytics engineers and provide technical guidance
    • Lead cross-functional collaboration with data scientists, analysts, and business stakeholders
    • Drive data quality initiatives and implement advanced monitoring systems
    • Evaluate and recommend new analytics engineering tools and technologies

    Required Skills

    • Data Engineering Practitioner
    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Statistical Methods Practitioner
    • Data Visualisation Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
