  • Head of Incident Management

    • The head of incident management provides strategic leadership for all incident management activities across the organisation. They establish enterprise-wide incident management frameworks and ensure organisational resilience and service reliability.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for incident management across the organisation
    • Establish enterprise-wide incident management frameworks and governance
    • Lead organisational resilience and service continuity initiatives
    • Drive cultural change towards proactive incident prevention
    • Represent incident management interests in executive and board-level discussions
    • Ensure alignment with regulatory and compliance requirements
    • Develop strategic partnerships for incident management capabilities

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Problem Management Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Consultancy Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Incident Manager

    Next role: Chief Information Officer or similar senior executive roles
