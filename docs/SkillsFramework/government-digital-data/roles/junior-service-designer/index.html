  • Junior Service Designer

    • A junior service designer helps design end-to-end services that meet user needs. They work under guidance to understand user journeys, map service touchpoints, and contribute to service improvement initiatives while learning about service design principles and methods.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Support mapping and analysis of user journeys and service touchpoints
    • Assist in conducting service design research and stakeholder interviews
    • Help create service blueprints and journey maps
    • Support service improvement workshops and co-design sessions
    • Learn about service design tools and methodologies
    • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams on service delivery

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Working
    • User Research Awareness
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Awareness
