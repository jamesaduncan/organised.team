  • Infrastructure Operations Engineer

    • An infrastructure operations engineer maintains and monitors production infrastructure systems, ensuring optimal performance, availability, and reliability. They support day-to-day operations and respond to infrastructure incidents.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Monitor and maintain production infrastructure systems
    • Respond to infrastructure incidents and performance issues
    • Perform routine maintenance and system updates
    • Implement monitoring and alerting for infrastructure components
    • Support capacity planning and performance optimization
    • Maintain infrastructure documentation and procedures
    • Collaborate with infrastructure engineers on system improvements

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Working
    • Programming and Build Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Awareness
