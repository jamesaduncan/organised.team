  • Lead Business Analyst

    • A lead business analyst is responsible for strategic business analysis across multiple domains and leads teams of analysts. They set standards, drive innovation in business analysis practices, and work at the most senior levels to influence organisational strategy and transformation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead business analysis strategy across multiple programmes and domains
    • Manage and develop teams of business analysts
    • Establish organisational standards and best practices for business analysis
    • Work with executive stakeholders on strategic transformation initiatives
    • Drive innovation in business analysis methodologies and tools
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through expert analysis
    • Lead major organisational change and transformation programmes

    Required Skills

    • Business Analysis Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • People Management Practitioner
    • Data Analysis Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Business Analyst
