  • Trainee Business Architect

    • A trainee business architect supports more senior business architect roles and receives training on business architecture tasks. They learn to understand organizational structures and business processes while working under supervision.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Support other business architects in producing clear business architecture outputs
    • Participate in workshops and meetings with stakeholders in the organisation
    • Learn and apply basic business architecture principles and methods
    • Assist in documenting business processes and organizational structures
    • Support data gathering and analysis for business architecture projects

    Required Skills

    • Business Architecture Awareness
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Awareness
    • Business Analysis Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
