  • Head of Accessibility

    • A head of accessibility leads the accessibility function across the organisation, setting strategic direction for inclusive design and ensuring compliance with accessibility standards. They manage accessibility teams, drive policy development, and champion accessibility at the highest levels of the organisation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement accessibility strategy across the entire organisation
    • Lead and manage accessibility teams and specialists
    • Establish accessibility governance, policies, and compliance frameworks
    • Champion accessibility at executive and board level
    • Build strategic partnerships with accessibility organisations and standards bodies
    • Oversee accessibility budget, resources, and capability development
    • Drive innovation and thought leadership in accessibility practices
    • Ensure organisation-wide compliance with accessibility legislation and standards

    Required Skills

    • Accessibility Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
