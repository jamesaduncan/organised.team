  • Junior Content Designer

    • A junior content designer creates clear, user-focused content for digital services. They work under guidance to write and design content that helps users complete their tasks, while learning about content strategy, user needs, and accessibility.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Create clear, accessible content for digital services and websites
    • Write user-focused copy for interfaces, forms, and service journeys
    • Support content audits and content mapping exercises
    • Help maintain content style guides and standards
    • Collaborate with designers and developers on content implementation
    • Participate in user research sessions to understand content needs

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Accessibility Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
    • User Research Awareness
