  • Senior User Researcher

    • A senior user researcher leads user research for complex programmes and services. They mentor junior researchers, establish research standards, and drive user research excellence while ensuring research insights inform strategic decisions and service design approaches.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead user research for complex, multi-service programmes
    • Develop research strategies and frameworks for organisational adoption
    • Mentor and develop junior user researchers and research capability
    • Establish user research standards, methods, and ethical practices
    • Lead cross-functional collaboration to embed research insights in decision-making
    • Drive innovation in user research tools, techniques, and methodologies
    • Provide expert guidance on complex research challenges and policy implications

    Required Skills

    • User Research Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Accessibility Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
