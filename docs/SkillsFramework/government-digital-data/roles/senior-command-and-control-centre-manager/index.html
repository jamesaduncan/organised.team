  • Senior Command and Control Centre Manager

    • A senior command and control centre manager leads strategic operational coordination activities and establishes best practices for operational centres. They drive continuous improvement in operational processes and develop organisational operational capabilities.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead strategic development of command and control centre capabilities
    • Establish operational procedures and best practices for multiple centres
    • Mentor and develop operational management staff
    • Drive automation and efficiency improvements in operational processes
    • Lead major incident coordination and post-incident reviews
    • Collaborate with senior leaders on operational strategy and planning
    • Support business continuity and disaster recovery planning

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Problem Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Consultancy Working
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
