  • Head of Content Strategy

    • A head of content strategy leads the content strategy function across the organisation, setting strategic direction for all content approaches and ensuring content strategy is embedded in organisational planning. They manage content strategy teams and drive organisation-wide content transformation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisation-wide content strategy and vision
    • Lead and manage content strategy teams and specialists
    • Establish content strategy governance and operating models
    • Champion content strategy at executive and board level
    • Build strategic partnerships with content and publishing communities
    • Oversee content transformation programmes and major initiatives
    • Drive innovation in content strategy tools, techniques, and approaches
    • Ensure content strategy alignment with organisational objectives and user needs

    Required Skills

    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • User Research Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
