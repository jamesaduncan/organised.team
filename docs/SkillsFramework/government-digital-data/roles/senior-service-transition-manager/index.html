  • Senior Service Transition Manager

    • A senior service transition manager leads complex service transition activities and establishes best practices for transitioning services into production. They mentor junior managers and drive continuous improvement in transition processes.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex service transition programs across multiple services
    • Establish service transition best practices and frameworks
    • Mentor and develop junior service transition managers
    • Drive automation and efficiency improvements in transition processes
    • Lead cross-functional transition teams and governance activities
    • Support strategic planning for service transition capabilities
    • Collaborate with architects on service design for operability

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Leadership Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • Strategy and Planning Awareness
