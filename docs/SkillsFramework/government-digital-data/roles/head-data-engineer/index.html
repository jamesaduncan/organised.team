  • Head of Data Engineering

    • A head of data engineering provides executive leadership for data engineering capability across the organisation. They set the strategic vision for data infrastructure, lead organisational transformation, and ensure data engineering aligns with business objectives at the highest level.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Set strategic vision and direction for organisational data engineering capability
    • Lead organisational transformation and major data platform modernisation
    • Build and manage data engineering function across multiple teams and domains
    • Partner with C-level executives to align data strategy with business objectives
    • Drive enterprise-wide data governance and architecture decisions
    • Represent the organisation in industry forums and strategic partnerships
    • Manage budgets, resources, and strategic investments in data technology

    Required Skills

    • Data Engineering Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Practitioner
    • Consultancy Practitioner
