  • Head of Delivery

    • A head of delivery is responsible for delivery strategy across an organisation, leading delivery capability and establishing organisational standards. They manage senior delivery teams and work at executive level to drive transformation and innovation in delivery practices.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement organisational delivery strategy and standards
    • Lead and manage teams of delivery managers across multiple programmes
    • Work with executive leadership on strategic transformation initiatives
    • Drive innovation and continuous improvement in delivery practices
    • Establish delivery capability and maturity across the organisation
    • Influence policy and strategic direction through delivery expertise
    • Manage organisational delivery risks and dependencies

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • People Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • User Focus Working
    • Product Management Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Delivery Manager
