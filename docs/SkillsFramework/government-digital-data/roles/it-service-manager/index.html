  • IT Service Manager

    • An IT service manager is responsible for the end-to-end delivery of IT services to business users. They ensure services meet agreed service levels, manage service relationships, and drive continuous service improvement.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Manage end-to-end IT service delivery and performance
    • Ensure services meet agreed service level agreements
    • Build and maintain relationships with business stakeholders
    • Coordinate service improvement initiatives and projects
    • Manage service budgets and financial performance
    • Lead service reviews and governance activities
    • Support service transition and change activities

    Required Skills

    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • User Focus Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
