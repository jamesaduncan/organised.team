  • Infrastructure Engineer

    • An infrastructure engineer designs, implements, and maintains IT infrastructure systems including servers, networks, storage, and cloud platforms. They ensure infrastructure reliability, security, and performance to support business operations.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design and implement infrastructure solutions for servers, networks, and storage
    • Maintain and monitor infrastructure systems for performance and availability
    • Implement infrastructure security measures and compliance requirements
    • Support cloud migration and hybrid infrastructure initiatives
    • Automate infrastructure deployment and configuration management
    • Troubleshoot and resolve infrastructure issues and incidents
    • Collaborate with development teams on infrastructure requirements

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Awareness
    • Consultancy Awareness
