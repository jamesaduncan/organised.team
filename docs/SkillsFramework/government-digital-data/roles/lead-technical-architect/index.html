  • Lead Technical Architect

    • A lead technical architect uses their expert knowledge and experience to direct work addressing high level and complex technical scenarios. They support and lead other technical architects, set strategic direction, and champion technical architecture across the organisation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead and direct the application of technical architectural principles, methods, and tools across the organisation
    • Establish technical architecture strategy and standards across the entire organisation
    • Provide expert consultancy on complex technical transformation challenges
    • Lead cross-organisational initiatives and major technical architecture programmes
    • Mentor and develop the technical architecture capability and community
    • Collaborate with senior leadership to align technical architecture with organisational strategy
    • Champion technical architecture best practices and maturity across the organisation
    • Represent the organisation in external technical architecture communities and forums
    • Drive digital transformation through innovative technical solutions and practices
    • Lead organisation-wide development platform and technical capability strategies

    Required Skills

    • Technical Architecture Expert
    • Software Development Expert
    • Technical Design Expert

    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
