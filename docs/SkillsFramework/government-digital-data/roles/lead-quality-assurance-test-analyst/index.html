  • Lead Quality Assurance Test Analyst

    • A lead quality assurance test analyst provides strategic leadership for testing capabilities across multiple teams and programmes. They establish organisational testing standards, drive innovation in testing practices, and ensure quality excellence across complex digital services.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for testing capabilities across multiple programmes
    • Establish organisational testing standards and quality frameworks
    • Lead innovation in testing practices and emerging technologies
    • Manage and develop teams of senior test analysts across projects
    • Drive quality excellence and continuous improvement initiatives
    • Partner with senior leadership on quality strategy and risk management
    • Champion user-centred testing practices and accessibility standards

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Problem Management Expert
    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Working
