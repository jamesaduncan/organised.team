  • Change and Release Manager

    • A change and release manager coordinates and manages changes to IT systems and services. They ensure changes are implemented safely, efficiently, and with minimal risk to service availability and business operations.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Manage the change management process and change advisory board activities
    • Coordinate release planning and deployment activities
    • Assess change risks and implement risk mitigation strategies
    • Ensure changes comply with governance and regulatory requirements
    • Coordinate with development and operations teams on release schedules
    • Maintain change and release documentation and metrics
    • Support incident resolution through emergency change processes

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Consultancy Awareness
    • Programming and Build Awareness
