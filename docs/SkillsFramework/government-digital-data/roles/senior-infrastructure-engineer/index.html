  • Senior Infrastructure Engineer

    • A senior infrastructure engineer leads complex infrastructure design and implementation projects. They establish best practices, mentor junior engineers, and drive strategic infrastructure initiatives including cloud adoption and modernisation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex infrastructure architecture and design initiatives
    • Establish infrastructure standards and best practices across the organisation
    • Mentor and develop junior infrastructure engineers
    • Drive infrastructure automation and DevOps adoption
    • Lead cloud migration and modernisation projects
    • Collaborate with enterprise architects on infrastructure strategy
    • Support capacity planning and infrastructure investment decisions

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Leadership Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Consultancy Working
    • Strategy and Planning Awareness
