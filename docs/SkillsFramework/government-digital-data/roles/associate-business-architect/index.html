  • Associate Business Architect

    • An associate business architect uses their knowledge and experience to address business scenarios. They continue to receive support and training for their role while taking on more independent responsibilities in business architecture projects.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Apply business architecture knowledge to address specific business scenarios
    • Recognise issues that cut across different areas of the organisation and present opportunities for improvement
    • Create basic business architecture artefacts under guidance
    • Facilitate stakeholder workshops and meetings
    • Support business case development with architectural input
    • Contribute to cross-functional project teams

    Required Skills

    • Business Architecture Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Consultancy Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Problem Management Awareness
