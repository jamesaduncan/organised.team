  • Senior Product Manager

    • A senior product manager leads complex product initiatives and provides expert guidance on product strategy and delivery. They mentor product managers and work with senior stakeholders to deliver strategic products that drive significant user and business value.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex, strategic product initiatives and roadmaps
    • Define product strategy aligned with organisational objectives
    • Mentor product managers and establish product best practices
    • Work with senior stakeholders on strategic product decisions
    • Drive innovation and continuous improvement in product practices
    • Lead cross-functional product teams and initiatives
    • Influence organisational strategy through product insights and data

    Required Skills

    • Product Management Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Data Analysis Practitioner
    • Mentoring Working
    • Problem Management Working
