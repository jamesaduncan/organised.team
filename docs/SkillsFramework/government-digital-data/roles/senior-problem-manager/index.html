  • Senior Problem Manager

    • A senior problem manager leads complex problem management activities and establishes best practices for problem resolution across multiple services. They mentor junior problem managers and drive strategic improvements in service reliability.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex problem investigations across multiple services and technologies
    • Establish problem management processes and best practices
    • Mentor and develop junior problem management staff
    • Drive proactive problem identification and prevention initiatives
    • Lead cross-functional problem resolution teams
    • Support strategic planning for service reliability improvements
    • Collaborate with architects on design for operational excellence

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Expert
    • Leadership Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Statistical Methods Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • Strategy and Planning Awareness

    Career progression

    Previous role: Problem Manager

    Next role: Head of Problem Management
