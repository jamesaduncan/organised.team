  • Graphic Designer

    • A graphic designer creates accessible, user-centred visual designs for digital government services. They work collaboratively with multidisciplinary teams to develop design solutions that meet user needs while maintaining brand consistency and accessibility standards.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design accessible visual interfaces for complex digital services
    • Create comprehensive design systems and component libraries
    • Collaborate with UX designers and developers on design implementation
    • Ensure visual designs meet accessibility and inclusion requirements
    • Develop brand guidelines and visual identity for government services
    • Test and iterate designs based on user feedback and research
    • Support design quality assurance across development cycles

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Accessibility Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • User Research Awareness
    • Strategy and Planning Awareness
