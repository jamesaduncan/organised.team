  • Lead Machine Learning Engineer

    • A lead machine learning engineer defines the strategic direction for ML engineering across the organisation. They establish enterprise ML platforms, drive ML innovation, and lead transformational initiatives in machine learning operations and deployment.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define strategic vision and roadmap for machine learning engineering
    • Lead enterprise-wide ML platform development and transformation
    • Establish organisation-wide MLOps standards and governance frameworks
    • Build and lead high-performing machine learning engineering teams
    • Drive innovation in ML technologies, tools, and methodologies
    • Partner with senior leadership to align ML capabilities with business strategy
    • Represent the organisation in ML engineering communities and industry forums

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Expert
    • Data Engineering Expert
    • Statistical Methods Practitioner
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Practitioner
