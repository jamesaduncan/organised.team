  • Test Manager

    • A test manager leads testing activities and manages quality assurance teams to ensure software meets user needs and quality standards. They coordinate testing efforts across projects, manage resources, and establish testing processes that support successful software delivery.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead and manage testing teams and quality assurance activities
    • Develop and implement test strategies and quality assurance plans
    • Coordinate testing activities across multiple projects and teams
    • Manage testing resources, budgets, and delivery timelines
    • Establish testing processes and quality standards
    • Report on quality metrics and testing progress to stakeholders
    • Support continuous improvement in testing practices and capabilities
