  • Senior Service Desk Manager

    • A senior service desk manager leads strategic service desk operations and establishes best practices for user support across multiple locations or services. They mentor junior managers and drive innovation in service delivery.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead strategic service desk operations across multiple sites or services
    • Establish service desk best practices and operational standards
    • Mentor and develop service desk management staff
    • Drive automation and self-service initiatives
    • Lead user experience improvement programs
    • Support strategic planning for service desk capabilities
    • Collaborate with other service managers on integrated service delivery

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Practitioner
    • User Focus Expert
    • Mentoring Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
