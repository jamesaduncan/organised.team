  • Senior Application Operations Engineer

    • A senior application operations engineer leads complex application support activities and mentors junior engineers. They design operational solutions, establish best practices, and drive improvements in application reliability and performance.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead complex application incident investigations and resolutions
    • Design and implement application monitoring and observability solutions
    • Mentor and guide junior application operations engineers
    • Establish operational best practices and standards for applications
    • Collaborate with architects on application operational requirements
    • Lead application performance optimization initiatives
    • Support strategic planning for application operations capabilities

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Expert
    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Leadership Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
