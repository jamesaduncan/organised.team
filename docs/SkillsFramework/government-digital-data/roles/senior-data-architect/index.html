  • Senior Data Architect

    • A senior data architect leads complex data architecture initiatives across multiple programmes or the organisation. They provide strategic direction on data architecture and serve as a subject matter expert.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead enterprise-wide data architecture strategy and roadmap development
    • Design complex, scalable data solutions for critical business systems
    • Establish data architecture standards, patterns, and best practices
    • Lead cross-functional teams on major data transformation programmes
    • Provide expert consultation on complex data architecture challenges
    • Mentor and develop junior data architects
    • Influence technology strategy and vendor selection decisions

    Required Skills

    • Data Architecture Expert
    • Data Modelling Expert
    • Strategic Thinking Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Data Governance Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Leadership and Guidance Practitioner
    • Consultancy Working
