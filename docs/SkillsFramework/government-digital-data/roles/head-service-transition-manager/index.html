  • Head of Service Transition Management

    • The head of service transition management provides strategic leadership for all service transition activities across the organisation. They establish enterprise-wide frameworks for service transitions and ensure seamless delivery of new services into production.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for service transition across the organisation
    • Establish enterprise-wide service transition frameworks and governance
    • Drive DevOps and continuous delivery transformation initiatives
    • Lead organisational capability development for service transitions
    • Represent transition management interests in executive forums
    • Ensure alignment with regulatory and compliance requirements
    • Develop strategic partnerships for transition automation and tooling

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Consultancy Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Service Transition Manager

    Next role: Chief Information Officer or similar senior executive roles
