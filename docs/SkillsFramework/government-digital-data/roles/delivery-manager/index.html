  • Delivery Manager

    • A delivery manager is responsible for the delivery of products and services, leading teams to deliver user-centred, high-quality solutions. They ensure effective agile delivery practices and remove blockers to enable teams to work effectively.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead multidisciplinary delivery teams using agile methodologies
    • Plan and manage product delivery timelines and milestones
    • Remove blockers and dependencies to enable team effectiveness
    • Facilitate agile ceremonies and maintain delivery cadence
    • Manage stakeholder expectations and communicate progress
    • Ensure delivery practices follow government standards and policies
    • Support continuous improvement in delivery processes

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • People Management Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • User Focus Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Product Management Awareness
