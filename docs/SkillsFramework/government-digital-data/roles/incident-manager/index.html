  • Incident Manager

    • An incident manager coordinates the response to IT service incidents, ensuring rapid restoration of services and minimal business impact. They lead incident response teams and maintain communication with stakeholders throughout incident resolution.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead incident response activities and coordinate resolution efforts
    • Manage incident communication with stakeholders and senior management
    • Escalate incidents appropriately and coordinate with specialist teams
    • Conduct post-incident reviews and implement lessons learned
    • Maintain incident management processes and documentation
    • Monitor service availability and performance metrics
    • Support business continuity and disaster recovery activities

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Leadership Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Consultancy Awareness
    • Business Analysis Awareness
