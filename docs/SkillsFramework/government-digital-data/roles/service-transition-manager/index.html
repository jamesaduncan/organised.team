  • Service Transition Manager

    • A service transition manager coordinates the transition of new or changed services from development into live operation. They ensure services are ready for production and that operational teams are prepared to support them effectively.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Coordinate service transition activities from development to production
    • Ensure readiness assessments are completed before service go-live
    • Manage knowledge transfer from development to operational teams
    • Coordinate training and documentation for new services
    • Support testing and validation activities during transition
    • Manage transition risks and issue resolution
    • Ensure compliance with governance and regulatory requirements

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Consultancy Awareness
    • Programming and Build Awareness
