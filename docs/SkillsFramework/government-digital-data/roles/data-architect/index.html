  • Data Architect

    • A data architect designs and implements data solutions that meet business requirements. They work independently on complex data architecture challenges and provide guidance to junior team members.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design comprehensive data architecture solutions for complex business requirements
    • Create and maintain data models, data flows, and integration patterns
    • Lead data governance initiatives and establish data standards
    • Evaluate and recommend data technologies and platforms
    • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand data requirements and constraints
    • Mentor associate data architects and support their development

    Required Skills

    • Data Architecture Practitioner
    • Data Modelling Practitioner
    • Data Engineering Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Data Governance Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Strategy and Planning Working
    • Problem Management Working
