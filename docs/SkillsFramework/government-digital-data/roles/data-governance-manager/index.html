  • Data Governance Manager

    • A data governance manager establishes and maintains data governance frameworks, policies, and processes to ensure data quality, security, and compliance across the organisation. They coordinate data stewardship activities and support data-driven decision making.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Develop and implement data governance policies and procedures
    • Coordinate data stewardship activities across business units
    • Monitor data quality and ensure compliance with data standards
    • Manage data catalogues and maintain data lineage documentation
    • Support data access requests and resolve data-related issues
    • Facilitate data governance committee meetings and decision-making
    • Provide training and guidance on data governance practices

    Required Skills

    • Data Ethics and Privacy Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Problem Management Working
    • Consultancy Awareness
    • Data Engineering Awareness
