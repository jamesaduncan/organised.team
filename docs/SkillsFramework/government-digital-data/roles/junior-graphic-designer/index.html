  • Junior Graphic Designer

    • A junior graphic designer creates visual designs for digital services and communications. They work under guidance to develop design assets, learn about accessibility and inclusive design principles, and contribute to the visual identity of government services.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Create visual designs for digital interfaces, websites, and applications
    • Develop design assets including icons, illustrations, and graphics
    • Support brand implementation across digital channels
    • Learn and apply accessibility principles in visual design
    • Collaborate with content designers and developers on implementation
    • Maintain design files and asset libraries

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Accessibility Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
    • User Research Awareness
