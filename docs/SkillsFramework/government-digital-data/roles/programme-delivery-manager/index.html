  • Programme Delivery Manager

    • A programme delivery manager is responsible for delivering complex programmes consisting of multiple projects and workstreams. They coordinate across teams, manage dependencies, and ensure successful delivery of strategic initiatives that deliver significant organisational value.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead end-to-end delivery of multi-disciplinary programmes
    • Coordinate multiple project teams and manage complex dependencies
    • Establish programme governance and reporting structures
    • Manage programme risks, issues, and stakeholder expectations
    • Ensure alignment between programme objectives and organisational strategy
    • Drive adoption of agile and lean delivery practices across the programme
    • Support change management and benefits realisation activities

    Required Skills

    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • People Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • User Focus Working
    • Product Management Working
    • Data Analysis Working
