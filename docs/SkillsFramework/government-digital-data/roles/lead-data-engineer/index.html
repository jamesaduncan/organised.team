  • Lead Data Engineer

    • A lead data engineer defines the strategic direction for data engineering across the organisation. They lead large-scale data platform initiatives, establish enterprise-wide data infrastructure standards, and drive innovation in data engineering practices.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and execute data engineering strategy and technical roadmap
    • Lead enterprise-wide data platform transformation initiatives
    • Establish organisation-wide data engineering standards and architecture patterns
    • Build and lead high-performing data engineering teams
    • Drive adoption of emerging data technologies and methodologies
    • Partner with senior leadership to align data capabilities with business strategy
    • Lead cross-functional collaboration with data science, analytics, and business teams

    Required Skills

    • Data Engineering Expert
    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Leadership Practitioner
    • Mentoring Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
