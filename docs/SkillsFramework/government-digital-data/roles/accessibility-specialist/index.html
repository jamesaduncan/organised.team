  • Accessibility Specialist

    • An accessibility specialist ensures digital services are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. They conduct comprehensive accessibility audits, provide expert guidance on inclusive design, and champion accessibility across the organisation.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead accessibility audits and testing of digital services across multiple platforms
    • Provide expert advice on WCAG compliance and accessibility standards
    • Design and deliver accessibility training to teams and stakeholders
    • Collaborate with designers and developers to implement accessible solutions
    • Conduct user research sessions with disabled users
    • Create accessibility documentation, guidelines, and best practices
    • Review and recommend assistive technologies and tools

    Required Skills

    • Accessibility Practitioner
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • User Research Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Agile Delivery Working
    • Strategy and Planning Awareness
