  • Lead Test Engineer

    • A lead test engineer provides strategic technical leadership for testing engineering capabilities across multiple programmes and teams. They establish organisational testing architectures, drive innovation in quality engineering practices, and ensure technical excellence across complex digital services.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic technical leadership for testing engineering across programmes
    • Establish organisational testing architectures and technical standards
    • Drive innovation and research in quality engineering and testing technologies
    • Lead and manage teams of senior test engineers across multiple projects
    • Partner with technical leadership on quality engineering strategy
    • Champion continuous improvement and technical excellence in testing
    • Influence technology strategy through testing and quality engineering expertise

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Expert
    • Agile Delivery Expert
    • Problem Management Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Accessibility Working
