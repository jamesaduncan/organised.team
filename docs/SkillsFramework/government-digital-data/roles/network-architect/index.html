  • Network Architect

    • A network architect designs and implements network solutions that meet business requirements. They work independently on complex network architecture challenges while providing guidance to associate network architects and collaborating with other architecture disciplines.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design comprehensive network architecture solutions for complex business requirements
    • Develop network standards, policies, and architectural principles
    • Lead network capacity planning and performance optimization initiatives
    • Evaluate and recommend network technologies and vendor solutions
    • Collaborate with security architects to ensure network security compliance
    • Mentor associate network architects and provide technical guidance
    • Lead network transformation projects and migration strategies
