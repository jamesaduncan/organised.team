  • Enterprise Architect

    • An enterprise architect designs and maintains the organisation's target operating model and technology strategy. They work independently to align business strategy with technology capabilities and ensure architectural coherence across the enterprise.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Develop and maintain enterprise architecture frameworks and standards
    • Create business capability models and technology roadmaps
    • Lead architecture governance and review processes
    • Align technology solutions with business strategy and objectives
    • Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand strategic direction
    • Provide architecture guidance and mentoring to other architects
    • Evaluate emerging technologies and their potential impact

    Required Skills

    • Enterprise Architecture Practitioner
    • Strategy and Planning Practitioner
    • Business Analysis Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
