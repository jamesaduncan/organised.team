  • Senior Data Scientist

    • A senior data scientist designs and leads data science projects, providing guidance to others and taking ownership of complex analytical challenges.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Design and lead the implementation of data science projects
    • Work collaboratively with a range of experts in support of organisational objectives
    • Communicate effectively and challenge delivery plans and priorities
    • Provide guidance and mentoring to junior data scientists
    • Understand and apply data ethics principles in data science work

    Required Skills

    • Data Visualisation Practitioner
    • Programming and Build Practitioner
    • Statistical Methods Practitioner
    • Data Engineering Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Data Ethics and Privacy Practitioner
    • Problem Management Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Mentoring Working

    Career progression

    Previous role: Data Scientist

    Next roles: Lead Data Scientist or Principal Data Scientist
