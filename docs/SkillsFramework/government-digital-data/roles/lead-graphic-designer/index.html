  • Lead Graphic Designer

    • A lead graphic designer leads the visual design function across the organisation, setting strategic direction for visual design approaches and ensuring user-centred design principles are embedded throughout all visual communications and interfaces. They manage graphic design teams and drive organisational design excellence.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement visual design strategy across the entire organisation
    • Lead and manage graphic design teams and visual design specialists
    • Establish visual design governance, standards, and brand frameworks
    • Champion accessible and inclusive design at executive level
    • Build strategic partnerships with design and creative communities
    • Oversee design capability development and training programmes
    • Drive innovation in visual design tools, techniques, and methodologies
    • Ensure design excellence and user focus across all organisational programmes

    Required Skills

    • User Focus Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Accessibility Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
