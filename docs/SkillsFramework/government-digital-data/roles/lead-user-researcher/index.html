  • Lead User Researcher

    • A lead user researcher leads the user research function across the organisation, setting strategic direction for research approaches and ensuring user research insights inform organisational strategy and decision-making. They manage user research teams and drive organisational user-centred culture.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and implement user research strategy across the entire organisation
    • Lead and manage user research teams and research specialists
    • Establish user research governance, standards, and ethical frameworks
    • Champion user-centred approaches at executive and policy level
    • Build strategic partnerships with research and academic communities
    • Oversee research capability development and organisational research culture
    • Drive innovation in user research methods, technologies, and approaches
    • Ensure user research excellence and impact across all organisational programmes

    Required Skills

    • User Research Expert
    • User Focus Expert
    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Accessibility Practitioner
