  • Senior DevOps Engineer

    • A senior DevOps engineer leads complex automation and infrastructure initiatives, mentors junior team members, and drives the adoption of DevOps practices across the organisation. They have deep expertise in cloud platforms, automation tools, and software delivery practices.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Lead the design and implementation of complex CI/CD and automation solutions
    • Architect scalable and resilient infrastructure solutions across multiple environments
    • Mentor junior DevOps engineers and share knowledge across teams
    • Drive DevOps culture and practices adoption organisation-wide
    • Design disaster recovery and business continuity solutions
    • Lead incident response and post-mortem analysis
    • Evaluate and recommend new tools and technologies
    • Collaborate with security teams on compliance and governance

    Required Skills

    • Programming and Build Expert
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner
    • Problem Management Practitioner
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Practitioner
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Consultancy Working
    • Mentoring Working
    • Leadership Working
