  • Head of Problem Management

    • The head of problem management provides strategic leadership for all problem management activities across the organisation. They establish enterprise-wide problem management frameworks and drive cultural change towards proactive problem prevention.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Provide strategic leadership for problem management across the organisation
    • Establish enterprise-wide problem management frameworks and governance
    • Drive cultural change towards proactive problem prevention
    • Lead organisational learning and continuous improvement initiatives
    • Represent problem management interests in executive forums
    • Ensure alignment with regulatory and compliance requirements
    • Develop strategic capabilities for predictive problem identification

    Required Skills

    • Leadership Expert
    • Strategy and Planning Expert
    • Problem Management Expert
    • Consultancy Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Mentoring Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • Statistical Methods Expert
    • Business Analysis Practitioner

    Career progression

    Previous role: Senior Problem Manager

    Next role: Chief Information Officer or similar senior executive roles

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • SCS1 (Senior Civil Service 1)
    • SCS2 (Senior Civil Service 2)
