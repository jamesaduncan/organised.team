  • Chief Technology Officer

    • A chief technology officer provides strategic leadership for technology across the organisation. They are responsible for technology strategy, innovation, and ensuring technology solutions align with business objectives and user needs.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Define and execute organisation-wide technology strategy and vision
    • Lead digital transformation and technology innovation initiatives
    • Build and manage high-performing technology teams
    • Establish technology architecture standards and governance
    • Drive adoption of modern technology practices and platforms
    • Manage technology investments and vendor relationships
    • Ensure technology solutions meet user needs and business objectives
    • Represent the organisation in external technology forums

    Required Skills

    • Technology Strategy Expert
    • Leadership and Guidance Expert
    • Strategic Thinking Expert
    • Innovation and Emerging Technology Expert
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Expert
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Expert
    • User Focus Practitioner
    • Agile Delivery Practitioner

    Career progression

    This is typically a senior executive role

    Indicative Civil Service grades

    • SCS1 (Senior Civil Service Pay Band 1)
    • SCS2 (Senior Civil Service Pay Band 2)
