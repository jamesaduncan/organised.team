  • Junior User Researcher

    • A junior user researcher conducts user research to understand user needs and behaviours. They work under guidance to plan and conduct research activities, analyse findings, and communicate insights while learning about research methodologies and ethical considerations.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Conduct user interviews, surveys, and usability testing sessions
    • Support research planning and recruitment activities
    • Analyse research data and identify user insights
    • Create research outputs including reports and presentations
    • Learn and apply ethical research practices and accessibility considerations
    • Collaborate with designers and product teams on research integration

    Required Skills

    • User Research Working
    • User Focus Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Awareness
    • Accessibility Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
