  • Problem Manager

    • A problem manager identifies, analyses, and resolves the root causes of recurring incidents to prevent future problems. They work proactively to improve service quality and reduce the impact of incidents on business operations.

    Typical responsibilities

    • Identify and analyse recurring incidents to determine root causes
    • Implement permanent solutions to prevent problem recurrence
    • Maintain problem records and track resolution progress
    • Collaborate with technical teams on problem investigation and resolution
    • Conduct trend analysis to identify emerging problems
    • Support major incident reviews and post-incident analysis
    • Provide recommendations for service improvements

    Required Skills

    • Problem Management Expert
    • Business Analysis Working
    • Statistical Methods Working
    • Communicating between the technical and non-technical Working
    • Stakeholder Relationship Management Working
    • Consultancy Awareness
    • Agile Delivery Awareness
