  • Leadership and Guidance

    • Leadership and guidance involves providing direction, support, and strategic vision to teams and organizations while fostering professional development and driving positive change.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand different leadership styles and approaches, and recognise the importance of guidance and mentorship in professional development.

    2. Working

      You can provide guidance to junior colleagues, lead small teams or projects, and demonstrate leadership behaviors in your day-to-day work.

    3. Practitioner

      You can lead teams and departments effectively, provide strategic guidance, develop others' capabilities, and drive organizational change initiatives.

    4. Expert

      You can provide senior executive leadership, shape organizational culture and values, influence government policy, and drive transformation across multiple organizations.
