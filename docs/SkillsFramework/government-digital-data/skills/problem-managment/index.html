  • Problem Management

    • Problem management involves identifying, analysing, and resolving the root causes of problems to prevent future incidents and improve service quality and organisational performance.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can recognise problems and understand the difference between incidents and underlying problems. You can contribute to problem-solving activities.

    2. Working

      You can investigate problems using structured approaches, identify potential root causes, and implement solutions under guidance. You can document problems and their resolution.

    3. Practitioner

      You can lead complex problem investigations, perform root cause analysis, design comprehensive solutions, and establish processes to prevent problem recurrence.

    4. Expert

      You can establish problem management frameworks and methodologies, lead organisation-wide problem resolution initiatives, and mentor others in advanced problem-solving techniques.
