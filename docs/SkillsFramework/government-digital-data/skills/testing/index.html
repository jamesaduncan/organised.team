  • Testing

    • Testing involves designing and executing systematic processes to verify that software, systems, and services meet requirements, function correctly, and provide the expected user experience.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand testing principles and approaches, and recognise the importance of quality assurance in government service delivery.

    2. Working

      You can execute test cases, identify and report defects, and contribute to testing activities using established tools and procedures.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design comprehensive test strategies, manage testing projects, establish testing standards, and ensure quality across the development lifecycle.

    4. Expert

      You can lead testing strategy and practice across organizations, establish quality frameworks and culture, and drive innovation in testing approaches and tools.
