  • Data Architecture

    • Data architecture involves designing and managing the structure, policies, and systems that govern how data is collected, stored, integrated, and used across government organizations.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the principles of data architecture and recognise the importance of structured data management in government services.

    2. Working

      You can contribute to data architecture projects, understand data models and schemas, and apply basic data integration principles.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design comprehensive data architectures, create data models, and establish data integration patterns that support business objectives.

    4. Expert

      You can lead enterprise data architecture strategy, establish data standards and governance frameworks, and drive digital transformation through data architecture.
