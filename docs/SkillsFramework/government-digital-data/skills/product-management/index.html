  • Product Management

    • Product management involves defining, planning, and executing the development and delivery of digital products and services that meet user needs and achieve organizational objectives.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand product management principles and recognise the role of product management in delivering successful government digital services.

    2. Working

      You can contribute to product planning and delivery, gather user requirements, and support product development activities following established frameworks.

    3. Practitioner

      You can manage product roadmaps, define product strategy, coordinate product development, and ensure products meet user needs and business objectives.

    4. Expert

      You can lead product strategy across multiple products, establish product management frameworks, and drive product innovation in government contexts.
