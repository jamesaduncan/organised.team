  • Problem Management

    • Problem management involves identifying and addressing the root causes of incidents and service disruptions to prevent their recurrence and improve service quality and stability.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the difference between incidents and problems, and recognise the importance of root cause analysis in improving service quality.

    2. Working

      You can investigate problems using structured approaches, contribute to root cause analysis, and implement solutions to prevent problem recurrence.

    3. Practitioner

      You can manage complex problem investigation processes, coordinate problem resolution activities, and establish proactive problem identification methods.

    4. Expert

      You can design enterprise problem management frameworks, establish problem management culture, and drive continuous service improvement through systematic problem resolution.
