  • Mentoring

    • Mentoring involves supporting and guiding others in their professional development by sharing knowledge, experience, and expertise to help them grow their skills and advance their careers.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the value of mentoring and recognise opportunities to support colleagues. You can participate in mentoring relationships as a mentee.

    2. Working

      You can provide informal mentoring and guidance to less experienced colleagues, share knowledge effectively, and support others' learning and development.

    3. Practitioner

      You can establish formal mentoring relationships, develop others systematically, provide constructive feedback, and help colleagues navigate career challenges and opportunities.

    4. Expert

      You can establish mentoring programmes, train others to be effective mentors, create mentoring strategies that support organisational capability building, and champion learning culture.
