  • Agile Delivery

    • Agile delivery involves using agile and lean methodologies to deliver working software and services in short iterations, responding to change, and continuously improving based on user feedback and learning.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand agile principles and practices, and work effectively within an agile team environment.

    2. Working

      You can apply agile methodologies like Scrum or Kanban, participate in agile ceremonies, and contribute to continuous improvement.

    3. Practitioner

      You can coach teams in agile practices, facilitate agile ceremonies, and adapt agile approaches to different contexts and challenges.

    4. Expert

      You can lead agile transformation, establish agile culture and practices across the organisation, and mentor others in advanced agile techniques.
