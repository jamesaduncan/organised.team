  • Accessibility

    • Accessibility involves ensuring digital services can be used by as many people as possible, including those with impaired vision, motor difficulties, cognitive impairments, learning disabilities, and deafness or hearing loss.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the importance of accessibility and recognise common accessibility barriers in digital services.

    2. Working

      You can apply accessibility guidelines (such as WCAG) in your work and conduct basic accessibility testing and audits.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design accessible services from the ground up, conduct comprehensive accessibility audits, and provide detailed guidance to teams on accessibility implementation.

    4. Expert

      You can lead accessibility strategy, develop accessibility standards and frameworks, and champion accessibility culture across the organisation.
