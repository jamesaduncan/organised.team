  • People Management

    • People management involves leading, developing, and supporting individuals and teams to achieve their potential while delivering organizational objectives and maintaining high performance standards.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the principles of people management and recognise the importance of effective leadership and team development in government contexts.

    2. Working

      You can manage small teams effectively, conduct performance conversations, support professional development, and handle basic HR processes and procedures.

    3. Practitioner

      You can manage larger teams and departments, develop talent pipelines, handle complex people issues, and create high-performing team cultures.

    4. Expert

      You can lead organizational people strategy, drive cultural change, establish people management frameworks, and influence HR policy across government.
