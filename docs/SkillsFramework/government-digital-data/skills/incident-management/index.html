  • Incident Management

    • Incident management involves responding to, managing, and resolving unplanned interruptions or reductions in quality of IT services to restore normal service operations as quickly as possible.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the importance of incident management and recognise common incident types and response procedures in government IT services.

    2. Working

      You can respond to incidents following established procedures, escalate appropriately, and contribute to incident resolution activities.

    3. Practitioner

      You can manage complex incidents, coordinate response teams, conduct post-incident reviews, and improve incident management processes.

    4. Expert

      You can design enterprise incident management frameworks, lead major incident responses, and drive continuous improvement in service resilience.
