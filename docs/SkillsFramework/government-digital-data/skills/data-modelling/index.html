  • Data Modelling

    • Data modelling involves creating abstract models that organize and define relationships between data elements to support business processes and analytical requirements.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the purpose of data models and recognise different types of data modeling approaches and techniques.

    2. Working

      You can create basic data models, understand entity relationships, and contribute to data modeling projects using standard tools and techniques.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design comprehensive data models for complex business requirements, optimize model performance, and ensure model quality and consistency.

    4. Expert

      You can lead enterprise data modeling strategy, establish modeling standards and best practices, and mentor others in advanced modeling techniques.
