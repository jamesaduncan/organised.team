  • Governance

    • Governance involves establishing frameworks, processes, and structures to ensure effective decision-making, accountability, and control across digital and data initiatives.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the importance of governance frameworks and recognise key governance principles and structures in government contexts.

    2. Working

      You can operate within established governance frameworks, follow governance processes, and contribute to governance activities and reporting.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design and implement governance frameworks, establish governance processes, and ensure compliance with governance standards and requirements.

    4. Expert

      You can lead enterprise governance strategy, influence governance policy, and drive cultural change around governance practices across organizations.
