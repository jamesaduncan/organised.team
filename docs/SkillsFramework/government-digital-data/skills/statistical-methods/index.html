  • Statistical Methods

    • Statistical methods involves applying statistical techniques to understand, analyse and draw insights from data. This includes hypothesis testing, regression analysis, and other statistical approaches to data analysis.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand basic statistical concepts and when different statistical approaches might be appropriate.

    2. Working

      You can apply common statistical methods to analyse data and understand statistical outputs from tools and software.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design and conduct statistical analysis, interpret complex statistical results, and choose appropriate methods for different types of data and research questions.

    4. Expert

      You can develop new statistical approaches, lead statistical strategy, and provide expert guidance on complex statistical challenges across the organisation.
