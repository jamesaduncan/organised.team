  • Enterprise Architecture

    • Enterprise architecture involves designing and maintaining the overall structure of an organisation's business processes, information systems, and technology infrastructure to ensure alignment with strategic objectives and efficient operations.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the role of enterprise architecture in organisational design and recognise how different architectural domains (business, data, application, technology) relate to each other.

    2. Working

      You can contribute to enterprise architecture activities, understand architectural principles and standards, and create basic architectural documentation under guidance.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design enterprise architecture solutions, lead architectural assessments, establish architectural principles, and ensure alignment between business strategy and technology implementation.

    4. Expert

      You can establish enterprise architecture strategy and governance, lead organisation-wide architectural transformation, and influence strategic decision-making through architectural insight.
