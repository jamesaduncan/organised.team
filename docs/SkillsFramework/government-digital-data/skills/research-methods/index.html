  • Research Methods

    • Research methods involves designing and conducting systematic investigations to gather, analyze, and interpret information to inform decision-making and policy development in government contexts.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand different research approaches and methodologies, and recognise the importance of evidence-based decision making in government.

    2. Working

      You can conduct basic research activities, gather and analyze data using established methods, and contribute to research projects under guidance.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design and execute comprehensive research projects, select appropriate methodologies, and produce high-quality research outputs that inform policy and practice.

    4. Expert

      You can lead research strategy, establish research frameworks and standards, and influence government policy through high-impact research and evidence.
