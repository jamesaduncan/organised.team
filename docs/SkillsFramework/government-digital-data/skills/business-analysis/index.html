  • Business Analysis

    • Business analysis involves investigating business processes, identifying requirements, analysing problems, and recommending solutions to help organisations improve performance and achieve their objectives.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the role of business analysis in projects and change initiatives. You recognise different types of requirements and basic analysis techniques.

    2. Working

      You can gather and document requirements, analyse business processes, and use basic business analysis techniques to investigate problems and propose solutions.

    3. Practitioner

      You can lead complex business analysis activities, facilitate stakeholder workshops, perform detailed gap analysis, and recommend comprehensive solutions to business problems.

    4. Expert

      You can establish business analysis methodologies and standards, lead enterprise-wide analysis initiatives, and mentor others in advanced business analysis techniques.
