  • Technical Architecture

    • Technical architecture involves designing the technical structure and components of systems, defining technology standards, and ensuring technical solutions align with business requirements and architectural principles.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand technical architecture principles and recognise the importance of architectural design in government systems and services.

    2. Working

      You can contribute to technical architecture activities, understand architectural patterns, and implement technical solutions following established architectural guidelines.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design technical architectures, establish technical standards, evaluate technology options, and ensure architectural compliance across projects.

    4. Expert

      You can lead technical architecture strategy, establish architectural frameworks and governance, and drive technology innovation across government organizations.
