  • Data Strategy

    • Data strategy involves developing and implementing comprehensive plans for how organizations collect, manage, and use data to achieve business objectives and create public value.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the importance of data strategy and recognise how data can support organizational goals and public service delivery.

    2. Working

      You can contribute to data strategy development, understand data requirements for specific projects, and align data activities with business objectives.

    3. Practitioner

      You can develop comprehensive data strategies, create data roadmaps, and establish frameworks for data-driven decision making across organizations.

    4. Expert

      You can lead enterprise data strategy across government, influence policy through data insights, and drive organizational transformation through strategic data initiatives.
