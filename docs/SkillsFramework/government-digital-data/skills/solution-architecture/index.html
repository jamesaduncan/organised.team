  • Solution Architecture

    • Solution architecture involves designing comprehensive technical solutions that address specific business requirements while aligning with enterprise architecture principles and government standards.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand solution architecture principles and recognise the role of solution architecture in delivering government digital services and systems.

    2. Working

      You can contribute to solution architecture activities, understand technical requirements, and help implement architectural solutions following established patterns.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design comprehensive solution architectures, evaluate technology options, and ensure solutions meet business requirements and technical standards.

    4. Expert

      You can lead solution architecture strategy, establish architectural frameworks and standards, and drive innovation in government solution design.
