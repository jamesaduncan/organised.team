  • Risk Management

    • Risk management involves identifying, assessing, and mitigating potential threats and uncertainties that could impact government operations, projects, and service delivery.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand risk management principles and recognise common risk types and their potential impacts in government contexts.

    2. Working

      You can identify and assess risks in your area of work, implement risk mitigation measures, and contribute to risk management processes.

    3. Practitioner

      You can develop comprehensive risk management frameworks, conduct risk assessments, and establish risk mitigation strategies across projects and programs.

    4. Expert

      You can lead enterprise risk management strategy, establish risk governance frameworks, and drive risk-aware culture across government organizations.
