  • Leadership

    • Leadership involves inspiring, guiding and influencing others to achieve shared goals, creating vision and direction, building high-performing teams, and driving positive change in organisations.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand different leadership styles and recognise effective leadership behaviours. You can take responsibility for your own work and support team objectives.

    2. Working

      You can lead small teams or projects, motivate others, delegate effectively, and take responsibility for team outcomes. You can adapt your leadership style to different situations.

    3. Practitioner

      You can lead complex teams and initiatives, create compelling vision and strategy, develop others, drive organisational change, and influence across boundaries.

    4. Expert

      You can provide executive leadership, shape organisational culture and values, lead transformation at scale, influence at senior levels, and develop leadership capability across the organisation.
