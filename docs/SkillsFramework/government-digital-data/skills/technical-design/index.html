  • Technical Design

    • Technical design involves creating detailed specifications and blueprints for technical solutions, defining system components, interfaces, and implementation approaches to meet functional and non-functional requirements.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand technical design principles and recognise the importance of detailed technical design in successful system implementation.

    2. Working

      You can create basic technical designs, produce technical documentation, and contribute to design reviews and implementation planning.

    3. Practitioner

      You can create comprehensive technical designs, establish design standards and patterns, and ensure designs meet performance and quality requirements.

    4. Expert

      You can lead technical design strategy, establish design methodologies and frameworks, and mentor others in advanced technical design techniques.
