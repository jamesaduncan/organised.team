  • Data Ethics and Privacy

    • Data ethics and privacy involves understanding and applying ethical principles and privacy regulations in data collection, processing, and use, ensuring responsible and lawful handling of personal and sensitive data.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand basic data protection principles and recognise when ethical considerations apply to data work.

    2. Working

      You can apply data protection regulations (like GDPR) in your work and identify potential ethical issues in data projects.

    3. Practitioner

      You can design data processes that comply with privacy regulations, conduct privacy impact assessments, and resolve complex ethical dilemmas in data use.

    4. Expert

      You can develop organisational data ethics frameworks, lead privacy strategy, and provide expert guidance on complex regulatory and ethical challenges.
