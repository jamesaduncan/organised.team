  • Data Governance

    • Data governance involves establishing policies, procedures, and standards for managing data quality, security, privacy, and compliance across government organizations.

    Proficiency Levels

    1. Awareness

      You can understand the importance of data governance and recognise key principles of data quality, security, and compliance.

    2. Working

      You can apply data governance policies in your work, ensure data quality standards, and understand data privacy and security requirements.

    3. Practitioner

      You can develop data governance frameworks, establish data quality processes, and manage data compliance across projects and teams.

    4. Expert

      You can lead enterprise data governance strategy, develop organizational data policies, and drive cultural change around data stewardship.
